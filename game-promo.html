The Story

Setting

Iraq, 2004–2007. A grounded war atmosphere where urban operations and rural missions blur the line between combat and consequence. This is where soldiers learn that war isn't black and white.

The Defining Moment

Checkpoint Twelve. A civilian family killed by mistake. Elias Haddad arrives too late. That moment shatters his simple view of warfare and becomes the foundation of every decision he makes afterward.

Elias "Reaper" Haddad

Arabic-American recon team leader. Calm, observant, disciplined. Haunted by guilt. Driven to protect lives, not chase glory. His moral compass guides his squad through impossible choices.

Marcus Kane

Second in command. Aggressive, force-driven. Challenges Elias's caution at every turn. Their conflict shapes the moral backbone of the campaign.

Daniel Ruiz

Younger squad member. Optimistic and inexperienced. Gives Elias a mentor and father-figure role—a reason to get the team home alive.

Gameplay Pillars

Squad Tactics

Command your team with precision. Stack up, breach, hold positions, cover sectors. Every order matters.

Stealth First

Detect enemy patrols, use darkness, coordinate sync takedowns. Avoid unnecessary firefights.

Intelligent Enemies

Enemy AI adapts—from unaware to suspicious to searching to alerted. They hunt as a team, just like you.

Realistic Combat

Weapon recoil, sway, suppression, injury pressure, ballistic danger. Every bullet counts.

Moral Stakes

Protect civilians. Minimize collateral damage. Make hard calls that define your character.

Squad Leadership

Keep your team alive. Mentor younger soldiers. Make decisions that will haunt you.

Operation Blackout

2:13 AM. Hostage rescue deep in enemy territory. Your squad must infiltrate undetected, make critical breach decisions, extract alive. One wrong move. One false order. And the hostage is gone.

Mission Parameters

Stealth-first infiltration under cover of darkness
Breach Choice: Silent or Explosive—each changes the mission outcome
Hostage recovery: Get them out alive
Extraction under pressure: Enemy reinforcements inbound
Failure condition: Hostage dies — mission over
