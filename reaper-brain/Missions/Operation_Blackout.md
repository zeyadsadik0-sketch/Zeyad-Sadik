# Operation Blackout

**Signature Mission | Stealth Hostage Rescue | 2:13 AM**

---

## Mission Brief

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Time** | 2:13 AM (pre-dawn darkness) |
| **Location** | Safehouse compound (urban area) |
| **Objective** | Rescue hostage alive |
| **Difficulty** | High (signature mission) |
| **Approach** | Stealth-first infiltration |
| **Duration** | ~45–60 minutes |
| **Squad Size** | Full team (Elias, [[Marcus Kane|Kane]], [[Daniel Ruiz|Ruiz]], 1 additional member) |

---

## Mission Overview

A high-value civilian hostage is held in a secure compound. Intelligence is limited but reliable. Enemy strength is estimated at 8–12 combatants. Time is critical — execution window is narrowing.

This is **Elias's moment** to prove that stealth, precision, and moral clarity can achieve objectives without unnecessary casualties.

---

## Phases

### Phase 1: Insertion (5–10 minutes)

**Objective:** Enter compound perimeter undetected

**Gameplay:**
- Rappel down from roofline
- Use darkness and wind as cover
- Avoid detection by patrolling sentries
- Position team at breach point

**Challenges:**
- Enemy patrol patterns are predictable but tight
- One missing sentry changes everything
- Weather (wind) can mask or amplify footsteps

**Tactics:**
- Sync takedowns available for paired sentries
- Suppressed weapons (silencers essential)
- Use cover shadows effectively
- Time movement to patrol gaps

**Player Choice:**
- Loud extraction = risk alerting guards, but faster
- Silent extraction = slower but maintains stealth

---

### Phase 2: Compound Infiltration (10–15 minutes)

**Objective:** Locate hostage, eliminate or bypass guards

**Gameplay:**
- Multiple entry points (windows, doors, roof access)
- Interior guard placement (guard barracks, communications center, hostage room)
- Civilian staff present (non-combatants)

**Challenges:**
- Guards are vigilant (Elias's intelligence is limited)
- Hostage's location not precisely known (requires reconnaissance inside)
- Time pressure increases (guards change shift, communication attempts)

**Tactics:**
- Gather intel via observation (scout rooms first)
- Use suppressed fire for isolated guards
- Coordinate squad positions to cover multiple areas
- Extract guards one at a time if possible

**Player Choice:**
- Aggressive sweep = faster, riskier, more noise
- Methodical approach = slower, quieter, controlled
- Hybrid approach = balanced risk/reward

---

### Phase 3: THE BREACH — Critical Choice Point

**Two Approaches. Same objective. Different consequences.**

#### Option A: Silent Breach
- **Method:** Pick locks, disable alarms, open doors quietly
- **Time:** 2–3 minutes per door
- **Noise:** Minimal (~10% detection risk)
- **Combat:** Unlikely during infiltration
- **Consequences:**
  - If successful: Hostage secured before alarm triggers
  - If discovered: Trapped inside with minimal exit options
  - Requires: Lockpicking tools, steady hand
  - Best if: Squad is full strength, time allows

#### Option B: Explosive Breach
- **Method:** C4 charge, explosive door ram, controlled detonation
- **Time:** 30 seconds per door
- **Noise:** Significant (alarm triggers immediately)
- **Combat:** Guaranteed firefight during extraction
- **Consequences:**
  - Hostage is reached before guards organize
  - Extraction becomes combat problem (but you control initial engagement)
  - Requires: Explosives, heavy weapons, cover positions
  - Best if: Squad prefers direct combat, time is critical

**Player Choice:** This decision reflects **Elias's philosophy:**
- **Silent = caution, precision, faith in stealth system**
- **Explosive = aggression, controlled chaos, speed**

**[[Marcus Kane|Kane's]] Perspective:**
- Advocates loudly for explosive breach ("Stop wasting time, Elias!")
- Will follow silently if Elias orders it, but is visibly tense
- May disobey if stealth infiltration goes wrong

**[[Daniel Ruiz|Ruiz's]] Perspective:**
- Trusts Elias's choice completely
- Nervous about either approach but ready to execute
- May comment on moral implications of either choice

---

### Phase 4: Hostage Recovery (5–10 minutes)

**Objective:** Secure hostage, manage panic, extract

**Gameplay:**
- Hostage AI is stressed, may panic or resist
- Guards converge on location (if alarm triggered)
- Extraction route may be compromised

**Challenges:**
- Hostage slows movement (non-combat AI)
- If alarm triggered: enemy reinforcements arrive every 60 seconds
- Hostage may be injured (speed/stealth compromised)
- Communication with team is critical

**Tactics:**
- Calm hostage (reduce panic speed penalty)
- Use suppressive fire to cover fallback
- Designate one squad member to escort hostage
- Maintain defensive positions while moving

**Player Choice:**
- Aggressive extraction = fast, risky, combat-heavy
- Careful extraction = slow, safer, stealth-focused
- Hybrid = balanced approach

---

### Phase 5: Extraction (15–20 minutes)

**Objective:** Exit compound, reach extraction point, survive

**Gameplay:**
- Enemy reinforcements converge
- Multiple exit routes possible
- Time pressure is high
- Squad cohesion matters

**Challenges:**
- If alarm triggered: Heavy resistance
- If stealth maintained: Light resistance, but time margin is small
- Hostage is slower (extraction route may need to account)
- Backup arrival time: depends on how long alarm has been active

**Tactics:**
- Use initial breach advantage (if explosive) to create distance
- Suppress enemy advance with suppressive fire
- Extract via most direct route (usually compromise with safety)
- Vehicle escape available at extraction point

**Player Choice:**
- Fast extraction = risky, combat-heavy
- Defensive extraction = slower, control enemy
- Multi-stage extraction = split team, risk squad

## Mission Outcomes

### Success Conditions
- Hostage extracted alive
- Team survives (optional: full squad survival)
- Extraction point reached

### Failure Conditions
- Hostage killed
- Entire squad killed
- Extraction point unreachable (time runs out)

### Performance Variations

| Approach | Stealth Success | Stealth Failure | Combat |
|----------|-----------------|-----------------|--------|
| **Silent Breach** | Perfect mission | Trapped, desperate firefight | N/A |
| **Explosive Breach** | High-speed combat extraction | Standard extraction | Controlled chaos |

- Use precision and timing
